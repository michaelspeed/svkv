Introduction

Swami Vivekananda Kendriya Vidyalaya is a brain child of Swami Vivekananda centre for Child & Youth Development. It was started in the year 2005 at Moran with a vision of National Regeneration through holistic and value- added education. Swami Vivekananda Centre is a non-profit, non- political, non-sectarian and spiritually oriented mission organisation affiliated to “ VIVEKANANDA TRUST” Founded by Padmashree Dr. H. Sudarshan, The founder of renowed “VIVEKANANDA GIRIJANA KALYAN KENDRA( V.G.K.K)”, B. R Hills, Karnataka.

The Centre aims in creating an awareness among all section of the society by promoting creativity and all round education amongst children and youth of Moran through a series of interventions like : Law cost, no cost science exhibition ,book exhibition , Personality Development Camps, Yoga And Pranayam Camps, Seminars on Environment, Health and Hygiene, Traditional Herbal Garden etc. Such increased sensitization of children, Youth, parents, teacher, and General public is very essential to bring about a new social order in our country .

In the year 2006, SWAMI VIVEKANANDA TRUST registered under Regd 429/2006, managed to establish a school called “ SWAMI VIVEKANANDA KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA” . The trustee body approached Government of Assam for allotting 6 Bighas (8026.56 Sq. Metre) land to established the School. We humbly express our gratitude to Sjt. Ashutosh Agnihotri (D.C) and all members of Land Advisory Committee (LAC) of the Dibrugarh district for allotting 6 bighas of land at Tiloinagar Napali Goan under Khowang Mouza of Dibrugarh district Vide reg.no - 18 dtd. 28/05/2008. The permanent School Building was constructed and running in force from the year 2013 at its allotted land Tiloinagar Moran.

School Prospectus

Swami Vivekananda Kendriya Vidyalaya, Moran is providing education based on multidisciplinary and creating happy learners.

The school believe that every child is unique in their way and we help them to bring inner strength and to nurture the child's potential and develop their intelligence and psychological power as envisaged by the great thinker and the philosopher-Swami Vivekananda.

The school is located in a remote area of Assam where education is not prevailed as it should have been. The student comes from lower middle class and poor families and the school is trying best to get them in the main stream so that they can compete with the rest of students who are from the big towns and cities. The school gives emphasis on moral values ethics and social skills.
